FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Stryde Client.


Does Stryde support offline accounts?+
Stryde supports both offline and online accounts. Some features and servers may require online authentication for full functionality and the best experience.
Is Stryde Client free to use?+
Yes — Stryde offers a powerful free tier with 30+ core modules, FPS optimisation, and community access. No credit card required. Just download and play.
When will Premium & Lifetime plans launch?+
Premium and Lifetime plans are currently in development and will be available very soon. We're putting the finishing touches on the advanced modules and bypass systems. Stay tuned on our Discord for the launch announcement.
Is Stryde safe to use on servers?+
Stryde is built with performance and stability in mind and is updated regularly to improve features and compatibility. Please note that using any third-party client is done at your own discretion — no client can guarantee complete safety.
Which Minecraft versions are supported?+
Stryde currently supports Minecraft version 1.8.9. Support for additional versions may be considered in the future — stay tuned on our Discord for updates.
How do I install Stryde Client?+
Currently, Stryde is available as a universal JAR file. A dedicated launcher for Windows and Linux is coming soon, with MacOS support planned for a future release.
How do I get support?+
The fastest way to get help is through our Discord server. You can also use the contact form on our Connect page. We typically respond within 3-4 hours on Discord and within 24 hours via email.
